Session review

What can be better

Your bench work matched plan, but accessory execution can be tighter and easier to recover from.

Workout feedback should summarize the whole day, not just one lift. That includes what matched plan, where execution slipped, and what matters most before the next session.

Cues for next time

  • Stay stacked and patient on the chest for competition bench.
  • Keep accessory work hard enough to matter, but not so sloppy it ruins recovery.
  • Use video review to decide whether the main issue is technical, fatigue-related, or programming-related.

Next programming change

Bench work stays on track, but the next pull day should stay capped if fatigue carries forward.

This feedback should bridge the gap between today's workout and tomorrow's programming so the athlete understands why anything changes.
